## 2. WHAT WILL BE COLLECTED?

### Passive Telemetry Data (Automatic Collection)
The FlowAI system will collect **non-invasive** workplace data:
- ⏱️ Window focus duration (how long you stay on one application)
- 🔄 Application switching frequency (how often you switch between apps)
- 💬 Communication latency (average response time in emails/messages)
- 📊 Deep work minutes (uninterrupted focused work periods)
- ⏸️ Break frequency (how often you take breaks)
- 🕐 Active work hours (total hours per day)

**Data NOT collected**: Passwords, personal files, screen content, browser history, or any sensitive information.

### Active Survey Data (Psychological Validation)
- **Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI-GS)**: 16-question psychological survey
  - Administered on: Day 1, Day 15, Day 30
  - Time: ~5 minutes per survey
  - Your responses help validate our AI model's accuracy
